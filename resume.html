A Primary School Excel App

Results Analysis Mark Book

Student Analysis Mark Book

The system, made in Excel, captures student marks, calculates class performance, produces a subject analysis, result analysis per class, and an overall school analysis.
It creates automated reports for each student and compiles a continuous assessment for each learner per term in that year.


The tool was designed as a replacement for a traditional manual Mark Book used in primary schools. The traditional method consisted of four books for capturing data, a tedious work that lasted a week, used a lot of stationery and very prone to errors.
